FAQ

Questions from Boston post-grads.

If you are busy, new to cooking, or unsure where to start, this page is for you.

Is this beginner-friendly if I barely cook?

Yes. Sessions are designed for beginners and include clear step-by-step guidance at each station.

Who usually attends?

Most attendees are recent grads and young professionals in Boston who want healthier routines and new social connections.

How many meals will I take home?

Most participants leave with around 5 ready-to-eat meals, depending on the specific session menu.

What does pricing look like?

Single sessions run around $39 to $42. We also offer a free first class through our University Pilot Pass and a lower-cost habit pack.

Is there a social component, or is it only cooking?

Both. The class is practical and structured, then there is optional social time after cooking with drinks and community networking.

How do I reserve my spot?

Go to the sessions page and send your preferred date by email. We confirm spots quickly and send a prep checklist.

Still have questions?

Reach out and we will help you pick the best first session.
